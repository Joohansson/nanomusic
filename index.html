How does it work?

Transactions/blocks are recorded live from the Nano network. Amounts are presented as floating text.
In each interval of 10 seconds a melody of max 64 notes is created by interpreting the block hash, type and amount.
For every new block recorded, the melody is extended and old notes are discarded.
If blocks arriving faster than the melody can be created, they are ignored.
